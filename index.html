Our Story

More Than Just a Cafe

BS Cafe is Ampati's beloved spot for great food, a warm atmosphere, and genuine hospitality. Nestled near the Rural Bank in the heart of town, we serve the community with love-crafted dishes blending local Garo flavours with popular Indian & Chinese favourites.

From star momos and crispy snacks to authentic Garo Traditional curry and refreshing beverages — everything is freshly prepared daily with quality ingredients and care.
