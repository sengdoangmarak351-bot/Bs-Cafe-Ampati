Our Story

More Than Just a Cafe

BS Cafe is Ampati's beloved spot for great food, warm atmosphere, and genuine hospitality. Nestled near the Rural Bank, we serve the community with love-crafted dishes blending local Garo flavours with popular Indian & Chinese favourites.

From star momos and crispy snacks to authentic Garo Traditional curry — everything is freshly prepared daily with quality ingredients and care. Love at First Bite — estd. 2021.
